## World at a Glance

**Halden City** sits between New York and D.C. — population ~8–10 million metro. Close enough to power to influence it, far enough to define itself. Over the last fifty years it has become a magnet for superhumans: heroes, villains, corporations, and agencies alike. Supers are tolerated, regulated, monetized, and occasionally celebrated, but never fully trusted.

### Districts

| District | Character |
|---|---|
| **Crownpoint** | Glass and steel. Corporate HQ, political offices, legacy hero monuments. Where the city projects power. |
| **The Ironworks** | Post-industrial sprawl. Shuttered factories, active shipyards, thinner law. Where masks feel personal. |
| **Riverside Ward** | Row houses, corner markets, crowded transit. Where most of Halden actually lives and pays for everything. |
| **The Glass District** | Pristine labs, private campuses, careful press releases. Every miracle casts a long ethical shadow. |
| **Monument Circle** | Grand plaza, statues of fallen heroes, government buildings. Where Halden tells itself what it wants to be. |
| **Blackwater Harbor (Dockside)** | Fog, shipping cranes, dark water. Cargo moves at all hours — some of it documented, some not. |

### Power Structure

| Organization | Role |
|---|---|
| **A.E.G.I.S.** | The Advanced Expert Group for Intervention and Security. Primary governmental agency for enhanced individuals, WWII roots. Medical, intelligence, oversight, containment. HQ beneath Crownpoint Federal Tower. |
| **The Alliance** | Halden City's premiere independent hero team. Paragon, Recluse, Riftfire, Quantum, Starfall, The Vanguard. Cooperative but not subordinate to A.E.G.I.S. |
| **Vane Capital Group** | Private infrastructure firm. Near-monopoly on Crownpoint construction. CEO Casimir Vane. Growth is suspiciously fast. |

## Tone & Feel

- Character-driven over action-driven
- Powers are metaphors as much as abilities — designed to say something about the person
- Heroism is exhausting; the city tolerates heroes, it does not worship them
- No one is purely anything
- The city has texture and memory

## How to Use This Wiki

- **Prepping a session or writing a scene?** Start with the relevant character file, then check open-threads.md.
- **Adding new canon?** Use the templates. Mark entries [CONFIRMED CANON] vs [WORKING ASSUMPTION].
- **Updated via Claude conversation?** Write the date and what changed at the bottom of the relevant file.
