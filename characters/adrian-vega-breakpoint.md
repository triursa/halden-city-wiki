## Background

Sandra Vega, Adrian's mother, was killed in 2006 during a botched early intervention by Riley Thomas (Recluse). Adrian was two years old. He has no memory of her — only photographs, his father's silences, and eventually the trail of evidence.

He spent years obsessively researching vigilante activity in Halden City, driven by one question: if someone had been there, could she have been saved? He uncovered Riley's identity. Instead of exposing him, he confronted Riley directly and asked to be trained. Riley agreed.

**Where Recluse fights because of guilt, Adrian fights because of clarity.** Their partnership is not one of debt or redemption — it is two people determined not to repeat the night that made them.

They are the most tactically precise partnership in the Alliance network.

---

## Character Profile

Adrian is steady. He processes hard things cleanly and keeps moving. He is a natural anchor for people around him — the person others lean on without fully realizing it.

He has a dry, relaxed humor and genuine warmth. He is not performing okayness; he is mostly okay, which is rarer than it sounds in this world.

His relationship with Riley is the defining mentorship in the story — but it is not a simple one. Riley trained him out of guilt and stayed out of genuine investment. Adrian knows more about what that night cost Riley than Riley realizes.
