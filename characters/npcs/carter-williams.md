## Who He Is

Carter Williams was paralyzed. Now he isn't. The Renegade Force healed him when it bonded, and that fact is the foundation of everything he believes about why he was chosen.

He is not wrong that he was wronged. He is not wrong that the party was someone's fault. He is being used by someone who understands his grievance better than he does, and that understanding is the trap.

His connection to Avi's backstory is the live wire of the "Who Decides" arc. The incident at the party paralyzed Carter. Avi does not know this. Riley knows. Signal knows.
