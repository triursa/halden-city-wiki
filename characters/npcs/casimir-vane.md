## Who He Is

A self-made billionaire raised in the Ironworks, Casimir Vane has a near-monopoly on Crownpoint construction contracts. His public persona is defined by philanthropic investment in law enforcement and public defenders — a tradition he traces to the night a detective told his family his father's killer had been found. He credits the Ironworks with teaching him everything worth knowing.

Industry insiders note that Vane Capital's growth has been unusually fast for a firm without obvious institutional backing. When asked, Casimir smiles and says the Ironworks taught him how to build things that last.

His sincerity about the city is not performance. He genuinely believes he is in its corner. He just believes in a version of the city that requires the current one to be dismantled first.
