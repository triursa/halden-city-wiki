## Powers

**Kinetic absorption and redistribution.**

Darcy absorbs incoming kinetic energy — impacts, shockwaves, force transferred through contact — and can redistribute it outward on demand. Practically: hits that should hurt her, don't. And what she sends back is proportional to what she absorbed, plus her own output.

She is extremely hard to put down through conventional force, and the harder someone tries, the more dangerous she becomes in response.

**Manifestation:** Triggered during a building fire — she ran into the structure when no one else would. The exact mechanism is unclear; she was caught in a partial structural collapse. The kinetic event of the collapse appears to be what activated the ability. She walked out carrying someone. She thought she had just gotten lucky.

She has not gotten any less capable since.

## Character Profile

Darcy is genuinely, non-performatively warm. She doesn't project toughness because she doesn't need to. She's not performing sunshine either — it's just how she is. She coaches kids because she's good at it and she likes it. She shows up the same way for everyone.

This unsettles Riley in a specific way: she has no resistance, no armor, and she doesn't need it. Riley's composure is load-bearing. Darcy's openness is structural. He doesn't know what to do with someone who's just... fine, and means it.

**The effect on Avi:** Avi notices how naturally Riley relaxes around Darcy. It reads as ease Riley doesn't extend elsewhere. This is a quiet wound in the dynamic — not dramatic, not spoken, just there.
