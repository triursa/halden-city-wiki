## Powers

Engineered as a cosmic envoy. Her capabilities are significantly beyond human baseline.

- Energy projection
- Limited matter restructuring
- Localized gravity manipulation
- Near-invulnerability
- Super-intelligence

She arrived with the directive to remove Earth from the cosmic record. She did not execute it. Whether the Auditors consider her a contained threat or an active problem is unknown.

---

## Background

She was made, not born — designed as a living instrument of the Auditors, the cosmic organization that considers itself the record-keepers and judges of civilizations. Her purpose was to assess and, when warranted, to act. Earth was deemed unstable. The determination was already made when she arrived.

Ethan Roberts intercepted her in orbit. He did not meet her with force. He asked her what she believed, rather than what she was made to conclude. She had never been asked that.

She defected. She has been marked "defective" since. She does not know if the Auditors will act on that designation or continue to observe. She has been carefully watching human life for years before engaging it directly. She is still calibrating.

---

## Character Profile

She thinks at a scale humans find uncomfortable. She has spent years watching this city, this team, these people — trying to understand how beings with such constrained lifespans and limited cognition manage to produce anything that justifies protection. She has found things that justify it. She has not shared the full list.

**The Sterling parallel:** Kiara defected from cosmic duty for her own reasons. Sterling Slate / Cosmic Knight defected from Darkstar for his. Two engineered beings who chose something their creators didn't intend. Kiara recognizes this. Whether she makes it explicit is her call. The resonance is there.
