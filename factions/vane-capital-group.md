## What Vane Capital Is

A private infrastructure firm with a near-monopoly on construction contracts in the Crownpoint District. Publicly, it is a success story: a self-made billionaire from the Ironworks who built something that transformed the city's skyline.

Industry insiders note that Vane Capital's growth has been unusually fast for a firm without obvious institutional backing. When asked, Casimir smiles and says the Ironworks taught him how to build things that last.
